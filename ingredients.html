The Pantry List

You don't need a warehouse of supplies. Here are the essentials to turn your kitchen into a pro soda shop.

The "Starter Pack"

With just these few ingredients, you can make 100% of our Top 15 Fan Favorites.

1. The Syrups

  • Coconut The foundation for almost every tropical or "Utah-style" drink.
  • Vanilla Essential for smoothing out energy drinks and creating "creamsicle" profiles.
  • Peach The #1 favorite for fruit-based colas and clear sodas.
  • Raspberry Provides the tartness needed for "berry" blends and the popular Dr. Pepper mixes.

2. The Fresh & Creamy

The "Acid" (Fresh Citrus)

  • Fresh Limes Essential for The Dirty Doc, The Founder, and The Loaded Diet.
  • Fresh Lemons Used in several refreshing Sprite and Ginger Ale mixes.

The "Dirty" (Dairy)

  • Sweet Cream Creamer The secret to the thick, marbled "cloud" effect.
  • Half & Half Used for a lighter, smoother texture in drinks like The Islander or The Founder.
