Strawberry Shortcake Dirty Soda Recipe

If you are looking for the ultimate dessert-in-a-cup dirty soda recipe, you've found it. Straight from the soda capital of the world in Provo, Utah, the Strawberry Shortcake is a rich, fruity, and ridiculously creamy upgrade to your standard Sprite. We aren't cutting calories here—this is pure, unapologetic indulgence.

What is a Strawberry Shortcake Dirty Soda?

Strawberry Shortcake is a dirty soda recipe made by combining Sprite, strawberry syrup, vanilla syrup, and a heavy float of sweet cream over pebble ice. The result is a vibrant pink drink that tastes exactly like a liquid version of the classic dessert.

The Flavor Profile

If you love rich, sweet drinks, this one is top-tier.

Vibe Dessert in a Cup 🍰
Sweetness 5/5
Creaminess 5/5
Tartness 1/5

What You Need (Ingredients)

To make this at your home soda bar, you'll need:

  • Sprite (12 oz can) — The crisp lemon-lime base cuts perfectly through the heavy syrups.
  • Strawberry Syrup (1 pump) — Torani works beautifully for getting that bright red base.
  • Vanilla Syrup (1 pump) — This gives it the "cake" flavor.
  • Sweet Cream — Heavy Float
  • Pebble Ice — Essential for the true soda shop crunch.

How to Make It (Step-by-Step)

  1. Fill the Glass: Pack an aesthetic glass cup to the brim with pebble ice.
  2. Add the Syrups: Pump your strawberry and vanilla syrups directly over the ice.
  3. Pour the Soda: Pour the Sprite to just below the rim of the glass.
  4. The Cream Float: Carefully pour a heavy float of sweet cream on top. Pro-tip: Let the cream sit for a few seconds before stirring so you can watch those satisfying white clouds sink into the pink soda.
  5. Mix and Sip: Give it a good stir with a long bar spoon and enjoy!
