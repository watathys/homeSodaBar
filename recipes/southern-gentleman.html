Southern Gentleman

If you are searching for the ultimate dirty soda recipe that feels like a slow summer afternoon on a front porch, you've found it. The KazziSoda team is coming to you live from the undisputed soda capital of the world—Provo, Utah—to drop a recipe that is as smooth as a jazz solo.

Meet the Southern Gentleman. This isn't just a drink; it's a mood.

What is a Southern Gentleman Dirty Soda?

The Southern Gentleman is a sophisticated twist on the classic dirty soda, featuring a base of Dr Pepper infused with peach and vanilla syrups. It is finished with a heavy float of sweet cream to create a rich, velvety texture that balances the bold spices of the soda with soft stone-fruit notes.

The Ingredients

To craft this masterpiece at your home soda bar, you'll need:

  • Dr Pepper: The spicy, 23-flavor foundation.
  • Peach Syrup: 2 pumps (adds that Southern sun-ripened glow).
  • Vanilla Syrup: 1 pump (for a smooth, floral finish).
  • Sweet Cream: A generous splash (heavy cream or coffee creamer works best).
  • Fresh Lime: (Optional) One wedge for the "Mix It Up" pro move.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Prep the Base: Fill a 24oz pebble ice cup (the only way to do it right) about three-quarters full.
  2. Flavor First: Pump your peach and vanilla syrups directly over the ice. This ensures the flavor is distributed from the first sip to the last.
  3. The Pour: Slowly pour your chilled Dr Pepper over the ice. Watch the carbonation dance with the syrup.
  4. The Float: Gently pour the sweet cream over the top. Personal Tip: Don't stir it immediately! Let the cream "cascade" through the dark soda for that perfect aesthetic marble look—it makes for a much better photo, and the first sip is incredibly indulgent.
  5. The Finisher: Squeeze in a fresh lime wedge if you want to cut the sweetness with a hit of bright acid.
