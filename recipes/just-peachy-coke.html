Just Peachy Coke

What is a Just Peachy Coke Dirty Soda?

A Just Peachy Coke Dirty Soda is a sweet and creamy beverage made by combining classic Coca-Cola with rich peach syrup and a luscious sweet cream float. This refreshing mix offers a Southern-inspired flavor profile that perfectly balances the crisp carbonation of dark cola with smooth, fruity indulgence.

The Vibe Check

Every drink we craft at KazziSoda has a distinct personality. The Just Peachy Coke brings major Southern Afternoon vibes. Imagine sitting on a wrap-around porch in the late July heat, but in a frosty glass.

Your Ingredients

To build this gorgeous drink, you'll need to gather a few simple staples for your home soda bar:

  • Classic Coke (or your preferred dark cola)
  • Premium Peach Syrup
  • Sweet Cream (half and half, or a coconut cream blend works beautifully)
  • Ice (pebble ice is highly recommended for that authentic soda shop crunch!)

Step-by-Step Instructions

Crafting this drink is all about the layers. Follow these steps for that flawless, photogenic swirl:

  1. Ice it up: Fill your favorite glass right to the brim with crushed or pebble ice.
  2. Add the flavor: Pour your desired amount of peach syrup directly over the ice.
  3. Pour the soda: Slowly pour your Coke into the glass, letting it mix naturally with the syrup while keeping those crisp bubbles intact.
  4. The Mixologist's Secret: Personal Tip: After pouring the soda, I highly recommend adding a quick pump of Vanilla Syrup before the final step. I tried this on a whim last week, and it completely transforms the flavor profile to taste exactly like a cozy bowl of Peaches & Cream Oatmeal. Trust me, it is an absolute game-changer!
  5. Float the cream: Gently pour your sweet cream over the top. Let it cascade down through the dark soda for that signature "dirty" look before giving it a good stir.
