Bullrider

If you are looking for an authentic, high-energy dirty soda recipe, you have found it because the KazziSoda team is broadcasting straight from the soda capital of the world: Provo, Utah. We know exactly how to engineer the perfect caffeinated pick-me-up. The Bullrider is a heavy, unapologetic sugar and energy rush built for pure, creamy indulgence.

What is a Bullrider Dirty Soda?

A Bullrider dirty soda is a highly caffeinated, creamy beverage crafted by blending the distinct, sharp flavor of Original Red Bull with sweet strawberry syrup and heavy coconut cream. This visually striking drink balances the classic, tart energy base with dense, fruity sweetness and a rich dairy finish, elevating a standard energy can into a complex, satisfying refreshment.

This specific drink channels The Utah Drive-Thru Classic vibe, bringing that iconic, long-line soda shop experience straight to your kitchen. Hitting a 4 out of 5 on the sweet scale, a mild 2 out of 5 on the tart scale, and maxing out with a heavy 5 out of 5 on the creamy index, it delivers a deeply satisfying, velvety mouthfeel. We are not cutting calories or pretending this is a health food. We are building a vivid, high-sugar, aesthetic masterpiece meant to be savored to the very last drop to push you through the longest days.

Sweetness 4/5
Tartness 2/5
Creaminess 5/5

Ingredients

  • Original Red Bull
  • Strawberry Syrup
  • Coconut Cream

Instructions

  1. Fill a heavy, clear glass to the very brim with soft, crunchy pebble ice.
  2. Pump the thick, sweet strawberry syrup directly over the bare ice, letting the red syrup pool at the bottom of the freezing glass.
  3. Pour the Original Red Bull over the iced syrup, listening to the aggressive fizz as the highly carbonated energy drink hits the glass.
  4. Squeeze a heavy layer of thick coconut cream directly over the top of the golden and red drink. Personal Visual Tip: Do not stir immediately. Watch closely as the dense, stark white coconut cream slowly fractures and sinks down through the carbonated Red Bull and red strawberry base, clinging to the pebble ice and creating a stunning, high-contrast visual gradient that is an absolute must-capture for a silent aesthetic video.
  5. Give it a steady, thorough swirl with your straw to fully integrate the heavy coconut cream with the sweet strawberry energy blend, and take that first icy, luxurious sip.

Mix It Up

Looking to change the flavor dynamics? Swap out the strawberry syrup for a generous pump of Peach Syrup. This simple adjustment instantly transforms the drink, yielding a creamy "Peach Ring" flavor profile. The smooth, candied peach notes perfectly complement the heavy coconut cream and sharp Red Bull, resulting in a nostalgic, gummy-candy finish that completely masks the bite of the energy drink.
