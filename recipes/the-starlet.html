The Starlet

Finding the perfect dirty soda recipe is a high-stakes art form, and the KazziSoda team is broadcasting from the global epicenter of the movement: Provo, Utah. If you're looking for a drink that matches the high-octane energy of a red carpet premiere, "The Starlet" is your leading lady.

What is a Starlet Dirty Soda?

The Starlet is a vibrant, dairy-free dirty soda that utilizes Alani Nu Cherry Slush as its carbonated base. It combines the nostalgic, candy-bright flavor of cherry with the smooth depth of vanilla and a sharp, citrus bite from fresh-squeezed lime.

The Flavor Profile

This recipe is for the bold. It skips the cream to let the tartness shine, resulting in a crisp, electrifying drink that wakes up the palate.

Vibe Hollywood Glam
Sweetness 4/5
Tartness 4/5
Creaminess 0/5

Ingredients

  • Base: 12 oz Alani Nu (Cherry Slush)
  • Sweetener: 2 pumps Vanilla Syrup
  • Acid: 1/2 Fresh Lime
  • Ice: Cold, crunchy pebble ice

How to Make The Starlet

  1. Pack the Ice: Fill a tall glass to the very brim with pebble ice. The tighter the ice, the more the flavors stay layered rather than immediately diluting.
  2. The Citrus Hit: Squeeze a heavy amount of fresh lime juice directly over the ice.
  3. Personal Visual Tip: Watch as the lime juice hits the pebble ice, turning the clear crystals into an opaque, frosted white. The sharp, zesty aroma is the first sign that this drink isn't your average soda.
  4. The Vanilla Base: Add two pumps of vanilla syrup. It will slide down the lime-coated ice, settling into the gaps and prepping the glass for the main event.
  5. The Neon Pour: Crack open the Alani Nu Cherry Slush and pour it slowly. The neon red liquid will swirl through the ice, catching the light and creating a glowing, jewel-toned finish that looks incredible under ring lights or kitchen pendants.

Mix It Up

For a truly cinematic experience, ditch the standard ice. Toss in a Frozen Berry Medley instead. The frozen strawberries, raspberries, and blueberries act as flavor-infused ice cubes, keeping the drink ice-cold while slowly bleeding deep purples and pinks into the cherry base as you sip.
