The Millionaire Dirty Soda Recipe

Creating the ultimate dirty soda recipe is practically a requirement when you're broadcasting from the soda capital of the world: Provo, Utah. Here at KazziSoda, we've mastered the art of the mix, and today we're sharing a rich, indulgent classic that proves why this dessert-in-a-cup trend is here to stay.

What is The Millionaire Dirty Soda?

The Millionaire is a rich, cola-based dirty soda that pairs the depth of classic Coke with indulgent caramel sauce and vanilla syrup. Topped with a heavy float of sweet cream, it delivers a deeply satisfying, salty-sweet flavor profile that tastes like liquid luxury.

The Vibe: Salty Sweet

This isn't a subtle drink; it's a full dessert experience. It's velvety, comforting, and unapologetically sweet, with just enough salt from the caramel to keep you coming back for another sip.

Sweetness 3/5
Tartness 1/5
Creaminess 3/5

What You'll Need

  • Pebble Ice: Crucial for the authentic home soda bar texture.
  • Classic Coke: One cold can.
  • Caramel Sauce: Look for a thick, rich sauce, not a thin syrup.
  • Vanilla Syrup: A standard simple syrup base.
  • Sweet Cream: Heavier than milk, creamy and rich.

Mixing The Millionaire

  1. Bottom Layer: In the bottom of a tall glass, add one heavy drizzle of caramel sauce and one pump of vanilla syrup. Stir them together until they form a golden-brown slurry.
  2. The Ice: Fill the glass to the absolute brim with crunchy pebble ice.
  3. The Cola: Crack open a cold can of Coke and pour it directly over the ice. Listen to that satisfying hiss as the carbonation hits the syrup base. Fill the glass about 90% full.
  4. Personal Visual Tip: For the best aesthetic, drizzle extra caramel sauce inside the glass, letting it run down the inner walls before adding the ice. It creates beautiful golden streaks that look incredible on camera as you pour the dark Coke in.
  5. The Float: Gently and slowly pour the sweet cream over the top of the Coke.
  6. Watch the Magic: Do not stir it immediately. Watch as the thick, white sweet cream slowly sinks into the dark cola, creating swirling, marble-like clouds. That visual is exactly what makes a soda "dirty." Give it a final, quick swizzle before drinking.

Mix It Up: The Billionaire

Upgrade this experience to a "Billionaire" by adding a heavy drizzle of chocolate sauce along with the caramel. The combination of chocolate, caramel, and Coke is reminiscent of a liquefied candy bar.
